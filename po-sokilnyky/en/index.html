About Us

The public organization «Sokilnyky» joined the movement of resistance to the occupiers. We are assisting the Armed Forces and citizens of Ukraine who suffered from the Russian aggression against Ukraine.

We deliver humanitarian aid by our own transport from the western border of Ukraine, to our logistics warehouse. Then we work on redistribution to meet the needs of servicemen and civilians in places of hostilities.
